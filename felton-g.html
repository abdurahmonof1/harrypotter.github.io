Tom Felton

Tom Felton is an English actor who played the role of Draco Malfoy in the Harry Potter film series. Draco is a student in Slytherin House at Hogwarts and serves as a primary antagonist to Harry Potter throughout the series. He is known for his pure-blood wizarding heritage, his rivalry with Harry, and his complicated relationship with his family and Voldemort.
