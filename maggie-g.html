Maggie Smith

Maggie Smith portrayed Professor Minerva McGonagall in the Harry Potter film series. Professor McGonagall is the Deputy Headmistress of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Head of Gryffindor House, and a skilled Transfiguration professor. Smith's portrayal captured McGonagall's strict yet fair demeanor, wisdom, and unwavering dedication to the welfare of her students and the protection of Hogwarts.
