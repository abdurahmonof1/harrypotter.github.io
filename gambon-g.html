Michael Gambon

Michael Gambon is an Irish-British actor who portrayed Albus Dumbledore in the Harry Potter film series from the third movie ("Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban") onwards, succeeding Richard Harris in the role. Gambon's portrayal of Dumbledore emphasized the character's wisdom, kindness, and formidable presence, guiding Harry Potter and his allies through their battles against the forces of darkness.
